Privacy Policy – Golf Society Hub

Last updated: 24 February 2026

Golf Society Hub (“the App”) is provided by Fairway Weather. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information – your email address and name, used for authentication.
  • Society data – members, events, tee times, and scores you create or manage within your society.
  • Optional images – profile photos or society logos you choose to upload.
  • Basic diagnostics – anonymous crash reports and usage statistics that help us improve the App (if collected by the platform).

2. Camera Permission

The App may request access to your device camera for optional features such as uploading a profile image or scanning QR codes. The camera is only activated when you actively choose to use one of these features and is never accessed in the background.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • Sync society data across your devices.
  • Generate exports such as tee sheets and score summaries.
  • Improve the App’s performance, reliability, and features.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored with trusted third-party cloud providers. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared only where required by law or to operate the services described above (e.g. cloud hosting providers).

6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@fairwayweather.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at support@fairwayweather.com.
